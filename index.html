Destination

Danau Beratan

Lake Beratan is the second largest lake in Bali, and is the source of irrigation for
rice fields and plantations across the entire Bedugul Village. The mountain on
which it sits is often referred to as “the holy mountain” as the weather is cool and the soil rich and fertile.



Khao Sok National Park

Khao Sok National Park in Southern Thailand is an amazing place. It is covered by the oldest
evergreen rainforest in the world, huge limestone mountains shooting straight up in the air,
deep valleys, breathtaking lakes, exciting caves, wild animals and much more.



Slovenske Tatry

The Slovenske Tatry, also known as the Slovakian Tatras or the High Tatras, are a breathtaking mountain range
located in northern Slovakia, forming part of the larger Carpathian mountain range in Central Europe.



Aconcagua

The highest peak in South America, located in the Andes mountain range in Argentina.
Climbing Aconcagua is a popular goal for mountaineers aiming to summit one of the Seven Summits.
